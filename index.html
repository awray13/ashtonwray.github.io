About Me

I'm an Entry-level front-end developer from Greensboro, NC. I'm a wife and mother of four very active children. I've just recently graduated from Guilford Technical Community College with my AAS in Information Technology, my focus being Web Development. I’m passionate about technology - not a specific framework or language. In this line of work it’s about adaptation, and what works best for the project. I’m open to learning any stack if it works.
My motto as a developer is if I am the smartest person in the room, I am in the wrong room. I love learning, and want to become the best developer I can be. I am a maker - I enjoy creating in my career and after hours. I believe in shipping quality code and working in flow. I care deeply about the code I write and the effect my work has on the product. I enjoy interaction with clients and seeing the joy they get when they get the application they’ve been wanting.
